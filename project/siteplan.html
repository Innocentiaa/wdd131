CenzonHub Website Plan

1. Site Name

CenzonHub

Inspired by amazon with my name "Centia" in short form of "Cen" to personalize my website, CenzonHub represents the ease of online shopping, providing customers with a convenient and stress-free shopping experience.

2. Site Purpose

CenzonHub aims to provide a seamless shopping experience by offering a variety of products, a user-friendly interface, and a reliable delivery service for busy individuals and small businesses.

3. Scenarios

Questions a user may likely ask:

  • What payment options are available on the site? The site accepts varieties of payment forms like Debit card, Prepaid card, Cash Transfer and all forms of Electronic Transactions.
  • How long will delivery take? Delivery time depends on so many factors which includes availability of products in your region,time of order and checkout time. Generally, Delivery should take 3-5 days.

4. Color Schema

The website will be designed with the following colors,

  • White(#FFFFFF): For background color to keep the site clean and readable.
  • Dark Midnight Blue (#003366): For navigation bars, buttons, and links.

5. Typography

The following fonts will be used for the Website,

  • Roboto: This will be used for headings to make them bold and clear.
  • Open Sans This will be used for body text for readability.
